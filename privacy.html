Privacy Policy

Effective 18 April 2026 · Mijenro International LLC

Mijenro International LLC ("Mijenro," "we," "us") respects the privacy of visitors to mijenro.com. This policy explains what information we collect when you browse the site or submit a partnership inquiry, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide. When you submit the contact form, we collect the name, business email, company name, target market, product category, estimated order volume, and any notes you choose to include. Providing this information is voluntary.

Information collected automatically. We use Google Analytics 4 to understand aggregate site usage. GA4 places cookies in your browser and records information such as pages visited, approximate geographic region (derived from IP, which is not stored by Google), device type, browser, and referrer. IP addresses are anonymized by Google before storage.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your partnership inquiry and arrange follow-up communications.
  • To send you a one-time confirmation email acknowledging that your inquiry was received.
  • To understand how visitors use the site so we can improve content and performance.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations.

We do not sell or rent your personal information, and we do not add inquiry submitters to any marketing mailing list.

3. Service Providers

We rely on a small number of reputable providers to operate the site. Information you submit may be processed by:

  • Resend — transactional email delivery (inquiry notifications and confirmation emails). Resend is based in the United States; see Resend's privacy policy.
  • Google Analytics 4 — anonymized site usage analytics. See Google's privacy policy.
  • Zeabur — hosting of the website and server runtime. Standard server access logs may be retained briefly for abuse prevention.

4. Cookies

The only cookies set on this site are those placed by Google Analytics for measurement purposes. No advertising, tracking, or third-party marketing cookies are used. You can block or delete cookies at any time in your browser settings; doing so will not affect your ability to submit an inquiry.

5. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained in our email inbox for as long as they remain commercially relevant to an active or potential partnership conversation. Analytics data is retained for 14 months in Google Analytics.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including residents of the EU, UK, and California), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal information, and to withdraw consent. To exercise any of these rights, email us at contact@mijenro.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. International Transfers

Mijenro operates offices in the United States and China. Information you submit may be stored or processed in either jurisdiction, and by contacting us you consent to such transfer.

8. Security

We use HTTPS throughout the site, industry-standard infrastructure providers, and keep the list of people with access to inquiry data strictly limited. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect the information entrusted to us.

9. Children

This is a B2B website intended for commercial audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this policy from time to time. The "Effective" date at the top will reflect the most recent update. For material changes, we will post a notice on the homepage for a reasonable period.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy or your personal information should be directed to:
Mijenro International LLC
500 7th Avenue, 8th Floor, New York, NY, USA
contact@mijenro.com

隐私政策

Mijenro International LLC（"美杰合"、"我们"）尊重访问 mijenro.com 的每一位用户的隐私。本政策说明我们在您访问本站或提交合作咨询时收集哪些信息、如何使用这些信息，以及您可以做出的选择。

一、我们收集的信息

您主动提供的信息。当您通过联系表单提交咨询时，我们会收集姓名、公司邮箱、公司名称、目标市场、产品品类、预估订单量以及您选择填写的备注内容。提供这些信息完全出于自愿。

自动收集的信息。我们使用 Google Analytics 4 了解站点的整体使用情况。GA4 会在您的浏览器中设置 Cookie，并记录诸如访问页面、大致地理位置（根据 IP 推断，Google 不保留原始 IP）、设备类型、浏览器、来源渠道等信息。IP 地址在存储前已由 Google 进行匿名化处理。

二、信息的使用方式

  • 回复您的合作咨询并安排后续沟通；
  • 向您发送一次性的确认邮件，告知咨询已被接收；
  • 分析访客如何使用本站点，以改进内容与性能；
  • 履行适用的法律义务。

我们不会出售或出租您的个人信息，也不会将咨询提交者加入任何营销邮件列表。

三、第三方服务提供商

  • Resend — 交易邮件发送（咨询通知与确认函），总部位于美国。
  • Google Analytics 4 — 匿名化的站点分析。
  • Zeabur — 网站与服务器托管。服务器访问日志仅为防止滥用短期保留。

四、Cookie

本站目前仅设置 Google Analytics 用于访问分析的 Cookie，不使用任何广告、追踪或第三方营销 Cookie。您可随时在浏览器设置中拦截或删除 Cookie，这不会影响您提交咨询。

五、信息保留

联系表单提交的内容将在与现有或潜在合作对话相关的期间内保留在我们的邮箱中。分析数据在 Google Analytics 中的默认保留期为 14 个月。

六、您的权利

根据所在司法辖区的法律（包括欧盟、英国及加利福尼亚州居民），您可能享有访问、更正、删除、限制处理个人信息以及撤回同意的权利。如需行使任何上述权利，请邮件联系 contact@mijenro.com，我们将在 30 天内回复。

七、国际传输

美杰合在美国与中国均设有办公地点。您提交的信息可能会在上述任一司法辖区存储或处理，联系我们即视为您同意该等传输。

八、安全

本站点全站启用 HTTPS，使用业内标准的基础设施服务商，并严格限制可接触咨询数据的人员范围。虽然互联网传输不可能 100% 安全，但我们已采取合理措施保护您的信息。

九、未成年人

本站点面向 B2B 商业受众。我们不会主动收集任何 18 岁以下人士的个人信息。

十、政策变更

我们可能不定期修订本政策。页首的"生效日期"将反映最近一次更新。对于重大变更，我们会在首页公告一段合理时间。
